A Love Poem

Chipi chip chapa chapa, cooking with a smile,
Doobi doobi daabaa, you make life worthwhile.
Maanya, my love, from Chennai's grace,
In my heart, you've found your place.

Explaining phages with care so bright,
Reading papers, your mind takes flight.
You cook for your mom, a queen in disguise,
With a heart so pure, and loving eyes.

Calligraphy flows from your graceful hand,
A future together, our love will expand.
Hopefully soon, as we plan ahead,
We'll marry and share the life we've led.
